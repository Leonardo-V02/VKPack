# Silent Gear Progression Pass - 2026-06-24

## What Changed

- Added 25 new real Silent Gear modifier traits for metallurgy, polymers, reactor materials, cascade alloys, and magic/resonance roles.
- Added 21 Silent Gear material definitions, including four staged cascade alloys and missing modded materials from Industrial Upgrade, Modern Industrialization, Blood Magic/Theurgy, Applied Flux, TFMG, and Electrodynamics aliases where appropriate.
- Rebalanced 189 existing materials with tighter intermediate/advanced ceilings and stronger family identity for conductive, bearing, carbide, radiant, resonant, void, dark, aluminum, and reactor materials.
- Added cascade alloy forge recipes plus matching Immersive Engineering arc furnace recipes.
- Added a starter Patchouli book: Gigani Gear Roadmap.
- Updated GrindingGear build metadata to expect 118 generated items and the expanded material/trait counts.

## Progression Shape

- Early: vanilla/Silent Gear basics, steel, aluminum, quartz, copper, and simple repairable support parts.
- Mid: Field Cascade Alloy, rubber/plastic/resin support, redstone-copper speed parts, cobalt-chrome and stainless durability.
- Late: Industrial Cascade Alloy, tungsten/nickel/tool-steel identity, carbide tips, nitinol recovery, aluminum-lithium handling.
- Endgame: Resonant Cascade Alloy, Terminus Cascade Alloy, reactor metals, superalloys, boss cores, void alloys, and Apotheosis socket planning.
- Quality chase: grade inputs, alloy them, grade the alloy output, then craft the tool part.
