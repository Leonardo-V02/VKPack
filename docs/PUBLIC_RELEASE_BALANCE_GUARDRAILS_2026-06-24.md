# Public Release Balance Guardrails

Date: 2026-06-24

This pass applies conservative balance polish for public release. It keeps specialty materials exciting while preserving a clearer difference between advanced and endgame material tiers.

## Applied Changes

- Capped non-endgame advanced material main-part harvest speed at 18.0.
- Left endgame materials untouched so ultra-late progression still has room to feel absurd and earned.
- Left lower durability/damage identity intact for fast light alloys and conductor alloys.

## Release Policy

- Intermediate materials should teach utility and mod compatibility, not dominate combat or mining.
- Advanced materials may specialize hard, but should not beat endgame materials across multiple major axes.
- Endgame materials may be excessive if they require late bosses, rare dimensions, high-tier machines, or expensive alloy chains.
- Weak metals and gems stay useful through tips, coatings, bindings, settings, linings, traits, or high enchant/magic roles rather than raw damage alone.
